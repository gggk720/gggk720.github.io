MapLog - Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 30, 2026

Overview

MapLog ("we", "our", "the app") is a travel map application. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

Information We Collect

  • Location Data: GPS coordinates are used to auto-detect countries you visit. Location data is stored locally on your device only.
  • Photos: Photos you add to your travel log are stored locally on your device.
  • Usage Data: Anonymous crash reports and basic usage analytics may be collected to improve the app.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide travel tracking and map visualization features
  • To auto-detect countries using GPS
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs

Data Storage

All personal travel data (visited countries, photos, routes) is stored locally on your device. We do not upload your personal travel data to any server.

Third-Party Services

The app may use the following third-party services:

  • Google Maps / OpenStreetMap for map rendering
  • Google AdMob for advertisements
  • Firebase Analytics for anonymous usage statistics

Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
