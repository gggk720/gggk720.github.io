Privacy Policy - I am The Coach: American Football

Last updated: March 13, 2026

Introduction

I am The Coach – American Football ("we", "our", or "the app") is an American football coaching board application. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

The app stores all data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.

Data stored locally on your device includes:

  • Team and player information you create
  • Formations, tactics, and lineup configurations
  • Training schedules and game records
  • Match statistics and performance data

Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data in accordance with Google's privacy policy. This may include:

  • Device identifiers
  • Usage data and ad interaction data

For more information about how Google uses data, please visit: Google Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Data Storage and Security

All user-created data is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this data. If you uninstall the app, all locally stored data will be deleted.

Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob - for displaying advertisements
  • Google Fonts - for font rendering

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
