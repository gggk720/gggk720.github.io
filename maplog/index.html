Features

GPS Auto-Detect

About MapLog

MapLog is a travel map app designed for adventurers who want to visualize their journeys. Whether you're a backpacker, a business traveler, or a family on vacation, MapLog helps you track every country you visit and relive your memories through beautiful map visualizations and animated travel routes.
